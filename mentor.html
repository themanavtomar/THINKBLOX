Science

Science encompasses the systematic study and analysis of the different natural occurrences and phenomena

through observation, theoretical and experimental evaluation.


Exams to consider:


  • JEE Main
  • NEET

Colleges and Degrees:

  • College 1 - B.Sc in Physics
  • College 2 - B.Tech in Computer Science

Commerce

Commerce is the second most popular one after Science.

The Commerce stream subjects comprise wide-ranging disciplines and areas that are focused on the world of Business, Economics and Accountancy


Exams to consider:


  • CA Foundation
  • B.Com Entrance Exams

Colleges and Degrees:

  • College 1 - B.Com (Hons)
  • College 2 - CA Program

Arts

The third and one of the most underrated streams after 10th is Arts/Humanities
which is a vastly immense discipline filled with a multitude of sub-disciplines and subjects.
It can be defined as an integrated field and Arts stream subjects encompass Humanities, Social Sciences, Liberal Arts, Performing Arts, Literature and Linguistics.


Exams to consider:


  • Common Entrance Tests for BA Programs

Colleges and Degrees:

  • College 1 - BA in Fine Arts
  • College 2 - BA in Literature
